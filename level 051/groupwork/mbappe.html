Kylian Mbappe

Early Life & Youth Career Kylian Mbappé was born in Bondy, a suburb of Paris, to a sports-oriented family. His father, Wilfried Mbappé, was a football coach, and his mother, Fayza Lamari, was a former professional handball player. From an early age, Mbappé was recognized as an exceptional talent, showing incredible speed and dribbling skills.

Youth Clubs:
AS Bondy (2004–2013) – Trained under his father at his local club.

Clairefontaine Academy (2011–2013) – France’s most prestigious football academy.

AS Monaco (2013–2016) – Joined Monaco’s youth system at 14.

At 15 years old, he was already being scouted by Real Madrid, Barcelona, and PSG. However, he decided to stay in France and develop at Monaco.

AS Monaco Career (2015–2017)
Mbappé made his professional debut for AS Monaco at just 16 years old on December 2, 2015, becoming the club’s youngest-ever first-team player.

Breakthrough Season (2016–17):
In the 2016–17 season, Mbappé exploded onto the scene, helping Monaco win Ligue 1 and reach the Champions League semi-finals.

Major Achievements with Monaco:
🏆 Ligue 1 Champion – 2016–17
⚽ 26 goals in all competitions
⚽ Scored a hat-trick in Ligue 1 at 18 years old
⚽ Scored 6 goals in the 2016–17 Champions League

Mbappé’s incredible performances attracted major European clubs, and in August 2017, he joined Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).

Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) Career (2017–Present)
Mbappé initially joined PSG on loan in 2017, but the deal became permanent in 2018 for €180 million, making him the second most expensive player ever (after Neymar).

Major Achievements with PSG:
🏆 7× Ligue 1 Titles (2017–18, 2018–19, 2019–20, 2020–21, 2021–22, 2022–23, 2023–24)
🏆 3× Coupe de France Titles
🏆 2× Coupe de la Ligue Titles
🏆 3× Trophée des Champions

Key Moments at PSG:
⚽ 2018 World Cup Aftermath – Became the face of French football at PSG.
⚽ 2020 Champions League Final – Led PSG to their first-ever UCL final (lost to Bayern Munich).
⚽ 2021 UCL Performance vs Barcelona – Scored a hat-trick at the Camp Nou in a 4-1 win.
⚽ 2022 PSG Contract Extension – Rejected Real Madrid to stay at PSG in a record-breaking deal.

With over 230 goals, Mbappé is PSG’s all-time top scorer.

France National Team Career
Mbappé made his senior debut for France in 2017 and has since become their main superstar.

Major International Achievements:
🏆 FIFA World Cup Winner – 2018
🏆 Nations League Winner – 2021
🥈 FIFA World Cup Runner-Up – 2022
🥈 UEFA Euro 2016 Runner-Up

Key World Cup Performances:
⚽ 2018 FIFA World Cup (Russia) – Scored 4 goals as a 19-year-old.

Scored in the final vs Croatia, becoming the youngest player since Pelé to score in a World Cup final.

Won Best Young Player Award.
⚽ 2022 FIFA World Cup (Qatar) – Scored 8 goals and won the Golden Boot.

Hat-trick in the final vs Argentina, but lost on penalties.

Became only the second player ever to score a hat-trick in a World Cup final.

Mbappé is expected to be France’s future captain and leader.

Playing Style & Strengths
Mbappé is one of the fastest and most complete forwards in football history.

✅ Pace & Acceleration – One of the fastest footballers ever, clocked at 38 km/h.
✅ Dribbling & Ball Control – Excellent dribbler, can beat multiple defenders.
✅ Finishing & Shooting – Deadly in front of goal, great with both feet.
✅ Versatility – Can play as a striker, winger, or attacking midfielder.
✅ Big Game Mentality – Performs in finals and knockout matches.

Individual Awards & Records
🏆 Golden Boy Award – 2017 (Best young player in Europe)
🏆 FIFA World Cup Best Young Player – 2018
🏆 Ligue 1 Player of the Year – 5× (2019, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024)
🏆 Ligue 1 Top Scorer – 6×
🏆 FIFA World Cup Golden Boot – 2022 (8 goals)
🏆 Only player to score a hat-trick in a World Cup final since 1966

Fun Facts
⚽ Big Cristiano Ronaldo fan – Grew up idolizing CR7.
⚽ Turned down Real Madrid in 2022 to stay at PSG.
⚽ Loves basketball and is friends with LeBron James.
⚽ Speaks fluent French, English, and Spanish.
⚽ Wants to become a club president after retirement.
