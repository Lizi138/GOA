About Booking

Booking at E-Learn is designed to be simple, transparent, and reliable. Whether you want to join a short workshop, enroll in a long-term course, or participate in live training events, our booking system ensures that your seat is guaranteed. We understand the importance of time and planning, and that is why we created an efficient platform where every student can secure their learning opportunities with ease.

Our booking system has been built with flexibility in mind. You can book for individual courses, package programs, or even reserve consultations with mentors. With just a few clicks, your journey toward knowledge begins. And because we value your trust, every booking comes with a clear confirmation message and transparent details about pricing, schedules, and refund policies.

Rules & Policies

To ensure a fair and consistent booking experience for all, we have established the following policies:

  • All bookings must be confirmed with full or partial payment.
  • Cancellations made 7 days prior to the course start are eligible for a 100% refund.
  • Late cancellations are eligible for rescheduling but not full refunds.
  • Each participant must use their own personal account for booking.
  • Seats are non-transferable unless officially approved by administration.

These policies are in place to maintain fairness and to help us deliver the best quality education without disruptions. By booking with us, you agree to follow these rules and respect other learners‚Äô opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I book multiple courses at the same time?

Yes, our system allows multiple bookings. You can add courses to your cart and complete them together.

2. What happens if a course is full?

You will be placed on a waiting list and notified immediately if a spot opens.

3. Can I change my booking date after confirmation?

Yes, rescheduling is allowed up to 48 hours before the course starts.

4. Do you provide discounts for group bookings?

Absolutely! Group discounts are available for organizations, families, or teams booking together.
