Lamine Yamal

🔹 Full Name:
Lamine Yamal Nasraoui Ebana

🔹 Date of Birth:
July 13, 2007 (He’ll turn 18 in 2025)

🔹 Place of Birth:
Esplugues de Llobregat, Barcelona, Spain

🔹 Nationality:
Spanish
(He is of Moroccan and Equatorial Guinean descent — his father is Moroccan, his mother is from Equatorial Guinea)

⚽️ Football Career
🏟️ Current Club:
FC Barcelona

Joined La Masia (Barcelona’s youth academy) at a very young age.

Considered one of the best talents to come out of La Masia in recent years.

🧒 Youth Career:
Displayed outstanding technical ability, dribbling, and vision from early on.

Skipped age groups due to his incredible maturity and skill.

Became a regular feature in Barcelona youth teams playing above his age.

🚀 Breakthrough at Barcelona
✅ First-Team Debut:
Date: April 29, 2023 (vs Real Betis in La Liga)

At 15 years and 290 days old, he became the youngest player to debut for Barcelona in La Liga in the 21st century.

Played as a right winger, showcasing flair, fearlessness, and high football IQ.

🌟 Style of Play:
Position: Right winger (but can play across the front line)

Strong foot: Left
Key attributes:
Exceptional dribbling
Vision and passing
Calmness under pressure
Incredible footballing intelligence for his age
Quick acceleration and creativity
Composed finishing
🇪🇸 International Career

Spain National Team:
Chose to represent Spain over Morocco.

Progressed rapidly through the youth ranks.

Made his senior debut for Spain in September 2023 in a Euro 2024 qualifier.

⚽️ Youngest to score for Spain:
Scored in his first full start — becoming Spain's youngest ever goalscorer at 16!

🏆 Achievements & Records
Youngest debutant for FC Barcelona in the 21st century.

Youngest goalscorer in Spain national team history.

Featured in UEFA Champions League at age 16.

Became the youngest player to assist in a Champions League match.

Part of Spain’s squad for Euro 2024 (if you're reading this in 2025, check how he performed — it was historic!).

🧠 Off the Pitch
Known for being humble and quiet, focused on football.

Barcelona is carefully managing his development to avoid burnout.

Already signed with top sponsors and has growing fame on social media.

🔮 Future Potential
Many experts and fans compare Lamine Yamal to:

Lionel Messi (for his creativity and left-footed brilliance),

Ansu Fati (in terms of debut trajectory),

Or even early Neymar (because of flair and unpredictability).

He's expected to be a core player for Barcelona and Spain for the next decade if he stays healthy and focused.
